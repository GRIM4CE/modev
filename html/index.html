Event Information

Dec 12-14

The Exo Health Winter Summit is a follow up event to the successful Exo Health Summit that took place May 25-27 in Aspen, CO.

This is the essential event for those who are seeking to drive exponential transformation through the use of new technologies and platforms. This schedule will feature executive leaders who will provide strategic insights into their digital transformation initiatives and future plans. The format will be shorter format talks as lead-ins to broader discussions that include the entire audience.

Dec 11

Modev is expanding it’s program this year with two distinct tracks - Wearables & Things and the Exo Health Winter Summit. Your registration will get you into both events. Space is limited so please register early.

The Wearables & Things conference will feature the world’s leading device, platform and app publishers focused on the healthcare industry. The schedule includes primarily technology leaders from the buy and sell side of the equation to share and discuss their successes, failures, product demos and the road ahead for 2017. Don’t miss this opportunity to learn from what some of the top companies are doing.

Dec 11

The third annual Global Digital Health Forum (formerly the Global mHealth Forum) brings together public and private sector organizations to share the latest evidence, experiences, and lessons learned on new applications and approaches in digital health. This two-day event is presented in partnership with the Global Digital Health Network and USAID
