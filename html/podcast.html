Hosts

Pete Erickson

Pete is the founder of Modev and creator of the Security by Design Community and Podcast Series. Pete has made a profession out of bringing together technology thought leaders and building community. Pete is the on-air "tech expert" for Fox 5 News in Washington, DC and resides in Arlington, VA with his wife and two kids.

Daniel Ford

Daniel Ford has built his career around information security management and has led major security iniatives at leading organizations such as Unisys, McAfee, XM Radio, Fixmo and Silent Circle. Dan is also a professor at Capitol Technology University and mentor at Mach37 Cyber Security Incubator.

Ryan Lelek

Ryan is a full-stack developer that takes products from the idea stage to full-scale deployment with hundreds of machines. In order to support and manage these clusters, he leverages automation and minimalism to create, test, monitor, and respond to daily events and keep customers happy.
